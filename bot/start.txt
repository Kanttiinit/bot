Use me to get lunch menus within telegram - powered by kanttiinit.fi

You can view my DNA at https://github.com/Kanttiinit/kanttiinit-bot
I won't gather any personal data.
I will ask for your permission if I request your location.

The following commands are available:

/food => interactive help for deciding where to eat
/<send location> => the closest 3 restaurants near you
/sub => posts the subway of the day
/txt [restaurantName OR ID] => text of lunch at the given restaurant
/menu [restaurantName OR ID] => image of lunch at the given restaurant
/img [restaurantName OR ID] => image of lunch at the given restaurant

/restaurants => a list of restaurantNames and IDs
/start => greeting + this text
/help => this text
/feedback [text] => sends a message with FRIENDLY feedback to the developers ;)
